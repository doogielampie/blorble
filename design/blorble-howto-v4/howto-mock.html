How to play

Blorbs love company. Help them into pods where nobody's the odd one out.

Every Blorb has 4 features: colour, eyes, shape, and antennae.

Colour
Eyes
Shape
Antennae
A pod is 3 Blorbs where each feature is the same on all three, or different on all three. No odd one out.
